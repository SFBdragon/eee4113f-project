### Additional Notes
- Ice floe size significantly reduced, melted, and broke apart;
- Buoy stand sank and buoy temporarily submerged during retrieval but collected and data retrieved.
- No camera footage recorded on GoPro Hero10 cameras (SHARC1 and SHARC2).
- Still images of conditions obtained from N Taylor

### Buoy Condition
- Power board disconnected after system disconnected.
- Condensation on exterior of seal and bottom PCB.
- Corrosion observed on power board lead solder pads and Iridium stand solder pads.
- Batteries ignited as they got wet during the retrieval
- **Cause:** AfriSol solder wire is susceptible to corrosion.
- **Battery voltage:** 7.195 V
